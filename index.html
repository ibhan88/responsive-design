About Us

As our name suggests, we are a group of camping aficionados based in Los Angeles, California. We are all about enjoying nature! We relax and unwind by breathing in fresh air, slowing down to admire the view, and finding connections with the earth. Of course, we also love sharing our food and keeping each other company too!

If you are interested in camping or nature in general or would like to meet new people, feel free to contact us!
